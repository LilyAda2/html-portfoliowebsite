The Best Bollywood Movies According to Lily Ada

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Jawan

Recently released in the last quarter of last year 2023, I will give the first one to Jawan because i still keep remembering the scenes in this movie. I love the action-packed vfx and most especially Mr.Khan.

My name is Khan

Really inspirational movie. Love the dialogue: 'My name is Khan and I'm not a Terrorist'.

Three Idiots

Love how the movie teaches us about the society as a whole, it's shortcomings. Love the comedy and music too.

Yeh Vaada Raha

I still keep watching this ever green movie - 'The Promose'. Though the movie didn't do well in India at that time (in the 80s) but for me it was a hit. Very emotional and romantic movie.
One of my first introduction to Bollywood movie in the 80s.

And now it's a wrap. These are my top picks i could think of . I have so many top picks but i would just settle for this ones.
